## Basics:

* It is recommended to make a medium-sized world for a single player playthrough as small-sized world offers little exploration, and less chance to get a certain items, and less fun.
* You'll start the game with a copper shortsword, a copper pickaxe, and a copper axe.
* Talk to the Guide for recipes.
* Try making a house as soon as possible, as you (and the Guide) can easily die when its night with starter equipments. A house requires a chair, a table, a lightsource, a torch, and walls.
* After making a house try finding iron/tin ores. It can be found on the surface.
* After that you can go spelunking by either finding a cave or just dig your way through it.
* Never go to the crimson biome, unless you get at least a tungsten armor set, and a ten or more damage weapon.
